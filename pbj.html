Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Description

This nutty and sweet delicacy is an American classic. Pair it with milk and say no more.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices White Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jam

Steps

  1. Toast slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on one side of toast
  3. Spread jam on one side of other toast
  4. Put toast together and enjoy! :)
